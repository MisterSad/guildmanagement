Frequently asked questions

Which game is this for?

The tool is built around the weekly event cycle of Foundation: Galactic Frontier-style guilds (SvS, GvG, Shadowfront, Defend Trade Route, Arms Race, Glory). If your guild plays these, you're home.

Do my members need accounts?

No. Only the guild leader (R5) and the officers (R4) log in. Members appear as roster entries — no sign-up, no personal e-mail needed.

Can I configure the reminder schedule?

Yes. Every server has its own battle times, so reminder schedules are configured per guild, in UTC, with sensible defaults you can edit.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, in one click, effective at the end of the billing period. No lock-in, no retention dark patterns.

What happens to my data if I stop paying?

Your guild switches to read-only for 30 days — nothing is deleted. You can export everything, and data is permanently removed only after a 60-day notice.

Is my data safe?

Each guild's data is isolated at the database level (row-level security), hosted in the EU, with daily backups. Only your R5 account can manage officers and settings.

What languages are supported?

English and French today; the app is built to add more languages quickly — tell us which one you need.
