Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Une version française sera également publiée.

1. Who we are

[LEGAL ENTITY] operates Guild Management Tool and acts as data controller for subscriber accounts, and as processor for roster data entered by guild leaders.

2. Data we process

  • Subscriber (R5): e-mail, hashed password, billing status (billing itself is handled by our payment provider).
  • Officers (R4): chosen identifier, hashed password.
  • Roster (entered by the guild): in-game pseudonyms, game UID, participation and score history, optional timezone. The guild leader is responsible for informing their members.
  • Push subscriptions: browser endpoint and keys, user-agent (no identity).
  • No advertising trackers, no third-party cookies. Analytics, if any, are cookie-less and aggregate.

3. Where & how long

Data is hosted in the European Union (Supabase). Active guild data is kept while the subscription lives; it becomes read-only for 30 days after lapse and is permanently deleted after 60 days, or sooner on request.

4. Your rights

Access, rectification, export and erasure: contact support@guildmgmt.app. Guild data export and full deletion are available from the app for the guild leader. You may lodge a complaint with the CNIL.

5. Processors

Supabase (database & hosting, EU), [payment provider: Paddle or Lemon Squeezy], [e-mail provider]. Data processing agreements are in place with each.
